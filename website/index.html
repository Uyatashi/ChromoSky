ChromoSky - A way to check the sky color

View the results from the photos you took from your phone or camera.

How it works

  • "Shoot" the sky

    Take a photo of the sky using our Android application. Then submit the data to our server.

  • Analyze the data

    The server analyzes the data and publishes its conclusions in this website.

  • View results

    View a google map with all the information for each location from which a picture was processed and analyzed.
